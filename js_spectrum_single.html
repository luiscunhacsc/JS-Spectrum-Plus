About This Emulator

Author: Luis Cunha, 2026.

Z80 microprocessor emulator core (`Z80.js`): Copyright (c) Molly Howell.

This emulator is dedicated to Pedro Gimeno, who first showed me the Turing machine principle in action on the day I saw a fully functional ZX Spectrum replica on my PC-AT display. The 286 could only run it at about 50% speed, but it felt magical. Pioneers like Pedro are often not recognized enough, and I want to openly manifest my respect and gratitude for his work.

For those who want to know more: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zzWyqmLdeS8
